## Full description (≤ 4000 chars)
```
Become a "new person" by filling your micro-moments with the words of Jesus Christ.

In 2017, a beloved prophet invited everyone to study everything Jesus said and did, as
recorded throughout the scriptures. The printed Latter-day Saint Topical Guide gathers
roughly 2,200 references about the Savior — but flipping to each one by hand makes that a
daunting task.

Scriptures: Jesus The Christ brings those references forward into the format we already
scroll every day: a continuous, social-style feed. Instead of an endless feed of noise,
every card is the Savior — His words, His works, His doctrine.

WHAT YOU CAN DO
• Scroll a continuous feed of scripture references about Jesus Christ.
• Read each verse in context, with the surrounding passages a tap away.
• Mark references as read and watch your progress grow as you work through them.
• Keep personal notes on any reference.
• Read in English or Spanish, with the official published text in both languages.
• Adjust the reading font size, light/dark theme, and more for comfortable study.

BUILT FOR FOCUS
There are no ads, no accounts, and no tracking. Nothing you do leaves your device. The
content is bundled in the app, so it works offline. It is simply you and the words of the
Savior.

A QUIET INVITATION
"If you proceed to learn all you can about Jesus Christ … your love for Him, and for God's
laws, will grow beyond what you currently imagine."

Fill your small moments with Him.

—
This app is an independent work. It is not an official publication of, nor endorsed by,
The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints.
```
