# Privacy Policy — Scriptures: Jesus The Christ

_Last updated: 2026-06-10_

Scriptures: Jesus The Christ ("the app") is published by **Molen Solutions LLC** ("we," "us").
This policy explains how the app handles your information. In short: **the app collects no
personal data and sends nothing off your device.**

## What we collect
**Nothing that leaves your device.** The app has no user accounts, no sign-in, and makes no
network requests to us or to third parties for analytics or advertising. The scripture content
is bundled in the app and read offline.

## What the app stores on your device
So the app can work, the following are saved **locally, on your device only** (in the app's
private storage):
- Your reading progress (which references you have marked as read).
- Notes you write.
- Your preferences (language, theme, reading font size).

This information never leaves your device, is not transmitted to us, and is removed when you
uninstall the app.

## Data sharing
We do not collect, sell, share, or transmit any personal information. The app contains no
third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking components.

## Children's privacy
The app is suitable for all ages and collects no personal information from anyone, including
children.

## Permissions
The app requests only the minimal permissions the platform needs to run and to store its own
local data. It does not access your contacts, location, camera, microphone, or files outside
its own storage.

## Changes
If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new
"Last updated" date.
